Create Year Slicer 17

To create the slicer select the slicer button on the visualizations panel. Select the year on the date table.

Turn on the slider header and select between on the drop arrow on the right. This enables the slider between the years of your data.

on the format make these adjustments;

X -1292

Y - 0

W  -  327

H - 138

slider header  -on (Then off after the between slider has been created),  Border - on,  Slider -on
